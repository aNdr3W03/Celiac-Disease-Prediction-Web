About

This project is one of the studies that departed from University of Sumatera Utara's undergraduate thesis research in computer science. This research focuses on the fields of machine learning, deep learning, and software engineering. Several points that are the advantages of this research are the synthetic oversampling technique which is applied to overcome unbalanced data labels or classes using Artificial Neural Network (ANN) architecture and its implementation in web-based applications and Android-based applications for the implementation of on-device machine learning.

The proposed research project is entitled, Implementation of a Celiac Disease Detection System using Deep Learning Methods and Synthetic Oversampling Techniques on the Web and Android-based Application. The team proposing this research project came from the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, University of Sumatera Utara, consisting of two lecturers as a research team leader and a research team member and also an undergraduate student as a research team assistant.
